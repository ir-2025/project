"I have a dream" speech

Why this speech is remembered today

During the March on Washington, Martin Luther King Jr was the last speaker of the event. His speech was around seventeen minutes long. In his speech he mentioned that he hopes our nation can grow and his children will not be judged because of their appearance. He also said he had faith in our nation rising and be equal to every citizen no matter their culture. Martin's speech is very known and iconic. His speech was a major part in the events successful protest. His speech was also one of the iconic moments in the civil rights movement. His speech will forever be remembered because of how inspirational it was. The march had 250,000 people attending which showed how many people supported the Civil Rights Movement and Martin Luther King Jr. This iconic speech helped Martin grow to get everyones attention. He wanted people to know why he wanted equality and freedom for African Americans.
